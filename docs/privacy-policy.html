Privacy Policy

Handball Manager

Last updated 13 July 2026  ·  App package com.handballmanager.mobile

Handball Manager is a single-player, offline sports management game. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you do in the game stays on your device.

This policy explains, in plain terms, how the Handball Manager mobile app ("the app", "we") handles your information. Because the app is fully offline, the short version is simple: we don't gather anything about you.

01Information we collect

None. The app does not ask you to create an account, does not request your name, email, location, contacts, or any other personal information, and contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising software.

02Data stored on your device

Your saved games, teams, and settings are stored locally on your device only, so your progress persists between sessions. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

You can remove all of this data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

03Data sharing and third parties

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data, because we do not collect any. The app makes no network connections to our servers and integrates no third-party analytics or advertising services.

04Distribution through Google Play

The app is distributed via the Google Play Store. When you download or update the app, Google may process certain information (such as your Google account and device details) under Google's own Privacy Policy. This processing is controlled by Google, not by us.

05Children's privacy

The app is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, no personal information from children is ever gathered.

06Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future — for example, if a feature that handles data is added — we will update this page and revise the "last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
